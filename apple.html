Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California, that designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services.

Main Products

About iPhone, Mac, Watch

  • iPhone

    iPhone (/ˈaɪfoʊn/ EYE-fone) is a line of smartphones designed and marketed by Apple Inc. The iPhone line of products use Apple's iOS mobile operating system software. The first-generation iPhone was released on June 29, 2007, and multiple new hardware iterations with new iOS releases have been released since.

  • Mac

    iMac: Consumer all-in-one desktop computer, introduced in 1998. Mac Mini: Consumer sub-desktop computer, introduced in 2005. MacBook: Consumer ultra-thin, ultra-portable notebook, introduced in 2006 and relaunched in 2015. MacBook Pro: Professional notebook, introduced in 2006. Mac Pro: Workstation desktop computer, introduced in 2006. MacBook Air: Consumer ultra-thin, ultra-portable notebook, introduced in 2008.

  • Watch

    The original Apple Watch smartwatch was announced by Tim Cook on September 9, 2014, being introduced as a product with health and fitness-tracking.It was released on April 24, 2015.

Products

The company's hardware products include the iPhone smartphone, the iPad tablet computer, the Mac personal computer, the iPod portable media player, the Apple Watch smartwatch, the Apple TV digital media player, and the HomePod smart speaker. Apple's software includes the macOS and iOS operating systems, the iTunes media player, the Safari web browser, and the iLife and iWork creativity and productivity suites, as well as professional applications like Final Cut Pro, Logic Pro, and Xcode. Its online services include the iTunes Store, the iOS App Store and Mac App Store, Apple Music, and iCloud.

iPad

Development of iPad

  • since 2010
    iPad series
    Published on January 27, 2010 at the Apple Inc. conference held at the Oubabu Art Center in San Francisco, USA. Positioned between Apple's smartphone iPhone and laptop MacBook series, there are only five buttons (Home, Power, volume plus & minus, and a gravity sensor and silent mode switch). The volume button layout is opposite to the iPhone. Provides functions such as browsing the Internet, sending and receiving emails, watching e-books, playing audio or video.
  • since 2012
    iPad mini series
    The iPad mini is a tablet designed, developed and sold by Apple. It was released on October 23, 2012 in San Jose, California. It is the first model in the iPad series with a 7.9-inch screen and the lightest and most portable model. The iPad mini features a 7.9-inch, 1024 × 768 resolution display, a 5 megapixel rear iSight lens, and a 1.2 megapixel front FaceTime HD lens. The hardware specifications of the processor are roughly the same as those of the iPad 2; however, the same Lightning as the iPhone 5 is used. The cable is used for charging or data transfer. In addition, the iPad mini has stereo speakers.
  • since 2013
    iPad Air Series
    Released on October 23, 2013, the screen size is 9.7 inches, lighter and thinner, with a minimum price of $499, which was launched on November 1, 2013. The new iPad continues to use a retina display with a narrower frame; the thickness is 7.5 mm, which is 20% thinner than the previous generation; the weight is 1 lb (about 454 g), and the previous generation weighed 1.4 lbs, which is nearly 30% lighter. In terms of internal components, the iPad Air tablet uses the Apple A7 processor and is equipped with the M7 coprocessor. The CPU processing performance is 8 times that of the previous generation, and the graphics processing capability is improved by 72 times.
  • since 2015
    iPad Pro Series
    Officially released at the Apple conference on the morning of September 11, 2015, the new iPad Pro features a 12.9-inch design with a resolution of 2732×2068, which can have a full-size keyboard on top, but the width and height of the previous Air. Basically similar. In terms of performance, the iPad Pro uses the A9X processor, which is twice the speed of the previous A8X. The graphics processor can compete with the current desktop computer, and the battery life can be up to 10 hours. The newly designed Cover is also added to the iPad Pro. In addition to the protection function, this is an external physical keyboard.
